5weet Productions Minecraft Server - Privacy Policy
Last Updated: 2/2/2024
At 5weet Productions, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you access and interact with our Minecraft server.
1. Information We Collect:
a. Minecraft Username: We collect your Minecraft username to personalize your gaming experience and enable you to interact with other players.
b. IP Address: We may collect your IP address for server security, analytics, and to prevent abuse.
2. How We Use Your Information:
a. Personalization: Your Minecraft username is used to personalize your gaming experience, such as displaying your in-game achievements.
b. Server Analytics: We collect data, including IP addresses, for server analytics to improve our services, monitor server performance, and enhance gameplay.
3. Information Sharing and Disclosure:
a. Third Parties: We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Your data is only shared within 5weet Productions for server management purposes.
4. Security:
a. Data Security: We implement security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption and regular security audits.
5. Children's Privacy:
a. Age Requirement: Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
6. Changes to Privacy Policy:
a. Updates: This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Users will be notified of any significant changes.
7. Contact Us:
a. If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at [support@5weet.uk.to].
By accessing and using the 5weet Productions Minecraft server, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
